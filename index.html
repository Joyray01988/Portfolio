About Me

I'm a SOC Analyst with hands-on experience in security operations, SIEM engineering, threat detection, and incident response across both banking and enterprise environments. I enjoy building SOC capabilities from the ground up - integrating servers, firewalls, and endpoints into SIEM platforms, creating effective detection rules, fine-tuning alerts, and developing practical playbooks that actually make teams more efficient.

I've worked extensively with IBM QRadar and Wazuh, as well as EDR/XDR tools, network security monitoring, cloud security controls, and File Integrity Monitoring.
